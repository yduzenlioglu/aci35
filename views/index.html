About ACI 35

Our team is an important part of the formation in our school which is called “ACI Garage”. ACI Garage is a project that is founded in order to make students experience their dreams beyond theoretical works. As ACI 35, we had our spirit from this “garage” and take off for our FRC journey with the Turkish Off-Season event that is held in Istanbul. We did great job by being one of the semi-finalist in this off-season experience. We may be a rookie team, however, we are looking forward to achieve more and more in the regional competition that will take place in New York.
